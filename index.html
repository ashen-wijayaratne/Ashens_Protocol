Ashen Protocol

version 2026.01-beta

Philosophy

This protocol is built on experimentation, consistency, and measurable outcomes. Everything here is based on what has produced real improvements in sleep quality, recovery, and daily performance.

Core Principles

  • Consistent sleep and wake times
  • Morning sunlight exposure within 30 minutes
  • Strict caffeine cutoff
  • Controlled evening light exposure
  • Cool sleeping environment

Daily Protocol

Morning

  • Wake: 07:00
  • Sunlight exposure
  • No phone for first 15 minutes

Evening

  • No caffeine after 13:00
  • Lights dimmed after 21:00
  • Sleep: 23:00

Metrics Tracked

  • Sleep duration
  • Sleep consistency
  • HRV
  • Resting heart rate

Notes

This is a living protocol. Adjustments are made based on observed data, not assumptions.
